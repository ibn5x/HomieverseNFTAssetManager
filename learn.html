The Crypto Homies

The Crypto Homies' is a pixel art NFT collection. They were created from the development team's original 90ies collection. Inspired to help bring Blockchain & Blockchain technology to the multitudes through Edutainment & development. Released in 'sets' called Gangs; Each 'Gang' contains 9 NFTs. There is a 'Special OG' mint in every set, which has a rarity rank ratio of 9:1. They're withheld from the open market, earmarked for events, raffles, and community rewards. Currently, we are developing a host of NFT game Dapps with enhanced play-to-earn game mechanics and interoperable smart contract integration for Crypto Homies NFT Collection. Our first NFT game for the Homieverse is entitled 'Tales From The Crypto Homies', join our Discord community to learn more.


Essential Information

  1. The Crypto Homies' is an Edutainment platform built around The Crypto Homie pixel art NFT collection, focused on developing Neolithic DeFi Instruments intuitively designed for the neophyte.
  2. The Crypto Homies are Non Fungible Tokens of the ERC721 variety. Each Crypto Homie is unique and indivisible.
  3. RESPK Tokens are ERC1155 tokens, the native asset for the Homieverse. Its token supply is capped, hardcoded at 10,000,000 RESPEK Tokens, with various use cases within the Homieverse. This token's logic gives it inherent deflationary properties. A portion of the total supply is burned for every Dapp transaction.
  4. The Crypto Homies are released in sets called Gangs. Each Gang contains 9 Crypto Homies. Each set has an OG Crypto Homie containing a rarity rank ratio of 9:1. OG Crypto Homies are withheld from the market and gifted to The Crypto Homie community.
  5. The Crypto Homies NFTs, game and homieverse is built on top of the Ethereum blockchain. The native asset of the Ethereum network, Ether, is required to cover transactions, including purchasing The Crypto Homie, adding to their story, and paying gas fees.
  6. The smart contract for the NFT game(s) is upgradable to anticipate new features should we choose to extend it. For our first upcoming NFT Game Dapp release, 'Tales From The Crypto Homies,' Only the holder of The Crypto Homie can add to the origin story of their Crypto Homie. The smart contract contains hundreds of ERC721 collectibles, some of which are Key items tied to a particular Crypto Homie. Suppose the holder adds to the origin story of their Crypto Homie utilizing the native token. In that case, a portion of the transaction fee is then used to mint escrowed collectibles, and the ownership is transferred to the holder. The holder can then sell, trade, or use their collectible throughout the Homieverse. The escrowed value is redeemable upon burning the collectible.

Homieverse Asset Manager

The Homieverse NFT asset manager is a graphical interface for our Homieverse NFT contract that allows users to govern their digital. Users can view all the NFTs within the contract, view the total amount of tokens in circulation, and view their token balance for a given asset. We have implemented a graphical interface for all the primary functions of our smart contract; these are the Mint, Burn, and Transfer functions.


Implemented Functionality


  1. Mint: Mints an additional ERC1155 token.
    Restrictions: Only owner of the contract can call the mint function; additional RESPK Tokens can not be minted.
  2. Burn: Transfers the users asset to 0 address.
    Restrictions: Only owner of an asset can call its burn function.
  3. Transfer: Transfers a digital asset to another address.
    Restrictions: Can only transfer owned assets.
